Player-Owned Farm Guide

Player-Owned Farm

To efficiently do player-owned farm on an ironman, you need to purchase a lot of unlocks. This guide will help you do that as fast as possible.

Before Level 64 Farming

  • Breeding Pen:
    • Breeding pair of Rellekkan cream rabbit in the breeding pen (can also use normal rabbits).
  • Small Pen:
    • Move rabbits that are bred into small pens.
    • Sell all rabbits except the breeding pair (preferably when adolescent).
  • Medium Pen:
    • 4 Sheep in each medium pen.
    • Sell all sheep (preferably when adolescent).
  • Large Pen:
    • 3 Bull in each large pen.
    • Sell all bulls (preferably when adolescent).
  • (You can also grow common green frogs on Anachronia.)

After Level 64 Farming

The objective now is to unlock the important POF rewards, in the following order:

Order Unlocks
1 Adam Antite
2 Medium Pen Breeding
3 Small Pen Breeding
4 Large Pen Breeding
5 Master farmer outfit blueprint
6 Master farmer fragment pack

Make sure that you send off Adam Antite until you have a breeding pair of red chinchompas, yaks, and dragons.

Pens Setup

  • Breeding Pen:
    • Obtain a breeding pair of spiders by killing spiders within the Lumbridge catacombs. Do this immediately at 64 Farming as this will be your main source of beans.
    • Place these spiders in the breeding pen.
    • If you have breeding yak or dragon, replace your spiders with those.
  • Small Pen:
    • Chinchompas (preferably red) in the small pens.
  • Medium Pen:
    • Move spiders that are bred into medium pens (do not place food in the medium pens until medium pen breeding is unlocked).
    • Sell all bred spiders (preferably when adolescent).
    • (Once medium pen breeding is unlocked, you can swap out 1 of your medium pens for a breeding pair of Zygomite (not gloomshroom). You do not need to feed them but can if you want.)
  • Large Pen:
    • Yaks > Dragons in large pens.

Once you have obtained all of the listed shop unlocks, you should stop selling animals as adolescent and instead let them grow to elders (to maximize XP).

Note

If you get a Strawberry/Vanilla/Chocolate cow from Adam Antite, bank them. You will need them for the Contract Claws mystery.
