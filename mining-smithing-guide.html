Mining and Smithing Training Guide

Introduction

This guide covers efficient training methods for mining and smithing skills, including the best locations, strategies, and tips for maximizing your experience gains.

Levels 1–20

Start by mining copper and tin ores in Burthorpe or Lumbridge. Smelt them into bronze bars and craft bronze items to progress quickly.

  • Location: Burthorpe Mine
  • Location: Lumbridge Swamp Mine
  • Tip: Use the anvil in Burthorpe for quick smithing training.

Levels 21–50

Mine iron ore and smelt steel bars. Use the steel bars to craft items like steel platebodies for fast smithing experience.

  • Location: Mining Guild
  • Location: Al Kharid Mine
  • Tip: Unlock the superheat spell for faster smelting.

Levels 51–99

Focus on mining higher-level ores such as mithril, adamantite, and runite, and smith items for profit and XP.

  • Location: Wilderness Mine
  • Tip: Use portable forges and anvils to maximize efficiency.
  • Consider mining at Seren stones in Prifddinas (level 89+) for AFK-friendly training.

Important Tips

  • Utilize mining and smithing dailies to gain extra XP.
  • Unlock the Mining Guild perks to increase mining efficiency.
  • Participate in Distractions and Diversions such as Shooting Stars for bonus XP.
